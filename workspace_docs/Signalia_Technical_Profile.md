## Entity Definition

**Signalia** is an automation boutique. Not an agency. Not a consultancy in the traditional sense.

The distinction matters. Agencies build deliverables. Consultancies give advice. Signalia builds systems that eliminate bottlenecks, automate decisions, and generate measurable output — then hands those systems over to clients who own them outright.

We work with founders, operators, and technical leads who are past the point of needing someone to explain what's possible. They need someone to execute it.

---

## Positioning

**Automation boutique and high-level engineering studio** serving businesses that have identified a leverage point and need it built.

We do not take on roadmap planning for clients who don't know where they're going. We engage when the problem is defined, the stakes are real, and speed is a requirement.

Our value proposition is not creativity for its own sake. It is **ROI on engineering time** — every engagement is designed to return more than it costs, measurably.

## Core Brand Attributes

**Direct.** We say what the engagement costs, what it delivers, and what success looks like before we begin. No ambiguity. No scope creep by omission.

**Expert.** Our team does not generalize. We specialize in three specific domains — web engineering, AI agent implementation, and agile delivery — and we do not expand that list because a client asks.

**ROI-oriented.** Every system we build is measured against a business outcome. Not lines of code. Not velocity points. Revenue recovered, hours automated, time-to-market reduced.

## Services

### Web Development

Full-stack engineering with a strong bias toward the modern Next.js/React/TypeScript ecosystem. Signalia builds production-grade web products — landing pages that perform, applications that scale, interfaces that convert.

**Current Stack Reference:**
- Framework: Next.js (App Router) with TypeScript
- Styling: Tailwind CSS v4
- Animation: Framer Motion
- Deployment: Vercel
- i18n: next-intl
- Scheduling/Booking: Cal.com embed integration

We do not build for maintenance dependency. Every delivery includes documentation, clean architecture, and handoff-ready code.

Performance benchmarks are set and measured. Lighthouse scores are tracked. We do not ship slow.

### AI Agent Implementation

Signalia designs, builds, and deploys AI agent systems for business processes that currently require human attention at scale.

This includes: automated research pipelines, CRM enrichment agents, outbound personalization systems, document processing workflows, and multi-step decision automations using LLM APIs (Anthropic, OpenAI) with tool use and context management.

We integrate with the MCP (Model Context Protocol) ecosystem — connecting agents to live business data in Notion, Slack, Google Workspace, HubSpot, Apollo, Linear, and other enterprise systems.

**What we deliver:** a production agent, its prompt architecture, its tool integrations, its monitoring setup, and a handoff brief. The client owns it. We do not manage it.

**Prerequisite:** The client must have a defined use case with a measurable outcome. "We want to use AI" is not a brief. "We want to reduce inbound triage time by 60%" is.

### Scrum Consulting

For engineering teams that are moving slowly and don't know why, Signalia provides embedded Scrum consulting: sprint design, backlog health review, velocity analysis, and team ceremony facilitation.

This is not a coaching engagement. It is a diagnostic-and-fix engagement. We come in, identify the structural dysfunctions in the delivery process, implement corrective sprint practices, and exit when the team is self-sustaining.

Typical engagement: 4–8 weeks. Deliverable: a team that ships predictably, a documented process, and a retrospective report.

## Engagement Model

**No retainers by default.** Signalia engages project-to-project with defined scope, timeline, and output.

**Discovery is not free fishing.** Every engagement begins with a paid scoping session that produces a written brief. If the project proceeds, that cost is applied to the engagement.

**We do not rescue bad projects.** If a codebase is beyond repair, we say so. We will recommend a rebuild with a defined scope. We will not bill hours on a foundation that cannot hold.

**Hand-off is always included.** The client leaves every engagement with working software, documentation, and the ability to maintain it independently. Dependency is not our business model.
